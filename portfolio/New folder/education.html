project

musical water foundain

A musical or dancing fountain is an animated water feature used in parks and public spaces for decoration and tribute. It combines water jets, sound sensors, lights, and music—synchronized through a microcontroller or computer—for dramatic effects. This paper presents a low-cost mini musical fountain using an Arduino board to control water valves and LEDs. Transistors act as switches, enabling water and light effects to pulse in sync with the music.
